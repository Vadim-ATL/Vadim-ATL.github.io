Research

I focus on controllable generation for medical imaging, specifically developing diffusion models that generate realistic medical images conditioned on clinical attributes. My work addresses data scarcity in medical AI by creating synthetic training data that improves diagnostic model performance.

Current interests: Latent diffusion models, multi-modal conditioning, downstream task validation, interpretability in generative models for healthcare.
